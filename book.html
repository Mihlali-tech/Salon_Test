Reserve your visit.

A short, considered process — choose your experience, refine it to your preference, and secure your appointment with a deposit. We'll confirm with you personally.

What to expect

  • I currently work weekends for most braided styles, with select weekday slots available for quicker styles like cornrows, free hand and treatments.
  • Longer protective styles — knotless braids, tribal braids and faux locs — are booked on weekends so there's no need to rush.
  • Available dates and times are shown once you've chosen your style.

Deposit & hairpiece

  • A 30% deposit secures every appointment, paid securely through PayFast. It's deducted from your total, with the balance settled on the day.
  • I arrive with my own Londie London hairpiece as standard. Prefer to use your own hair or a different piece? Just note it when you reserve, at no extra charge.
  • Please give at least 48 hours' notice for any rescheduling.
